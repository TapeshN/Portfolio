Charts Shared with Me

The Charts page shows a list of the charts. There are a few types: cardio, lifting, and summary charts. There are 2 sections: the user's charts, and charts shared with the user.

When the user (in this case, Daniel), wants to share a chart, he would click on the envelope to start. Check boxes will appear next to the date, and the user can check off which charts he wants to share.

After the charts are selected, the user is prompted to select which of his contacts he wants to share the chart.
